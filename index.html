# Your images unchained

The CDN is provided by Cloudflare(opens new window). Images are being cached and delivered straight from 190+ global datacenters(opens new window). This ensures the fastest load times and best performance.

# Widely used

On average, we resize 6 million (6×106) images per hour, which generates around 400TB of outbound traffic per month.
